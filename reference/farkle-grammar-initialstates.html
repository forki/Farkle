Farkle

InitialStates

Namespace: Farkle.Grammar

A structure that specifies the initial DFA and LALR states.

Record Fields

Record FieldDescription
DFA
Signature: DFAState

The initial DFA state. Instead of the LALR state, it is reset when a Token is produced.
LALR
Signature: LALRState

The initial LALR state. Instead of the DFA state, it is set at the beginning of the parsing process and persists for its entirety.
